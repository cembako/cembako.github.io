Cembako Apps

Cembako Cembako aims to create stability in market price, override the confusion in any crucial moments. It is both giving factual information of the commodities price analyzed by government, at the same time monitoring and ecting price in traditional market reported by society itself.
Features :
  1. Graph showing updates of official food price in DIY and comparison to the national's.
  2. User feedback via social media of the current market conditions in their neighborhood.
  3. User input of the food price they are facing at the traditional market.
  4. Alert notification when the market price exceed the official threshold national price.
  5. List of relevant news that might tell the cause of the abnormal market price.

Cembako can be a solution for instability of sembako market price.
